# Self Reminder

!!! Note

    Almost every December for the past decades, I tend to rush to make New Year's resolutions.

    Most of my plans were destined to fail. Some didn't make any sense, and the rest I would forget. Consequently, this leads to karmic anxiety.

    So, I want to start this year by first accepting my failures. It is okay for some or all of my plans to fail. My plans will change, and I accept that.

    Time is continuous. Clock measures itself.

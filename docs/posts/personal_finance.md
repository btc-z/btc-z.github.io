# Why New Year Resolution Fails You

Perhaps, my most habitual mental masturbation is to rash into planning new year resolution (or starting to have an anxiety of not writing one) towards end of a year. Yet, nearly 95% of them are destined to fail, causing more anxieties. Some of them don't make any sense, while others do but I later just forgot them about...

This year, I want to do somehting different. Instead of making new year resolutions, I want to accept the failure first. I won't accomplish all/anything I plan anyway. Besides time is continuous, why shall I treat it otherwise. Yes, the clock turns, and yes, we live in a solar system with a moon orbits around the earth, and the earth orbits around the sun, blah blah...

With failure accepted, I will record my attempts, and I will do so imperfectly, and that is ok.
